European timeline

From six countries to a political geography

European integration did not begin as a complete map. It began as a sequence of decisions: first coal, steel and a common market; then stronger institutions, European citizenship, the single currency, eastward enlargements and, finally, a first exit: the United Kingdom.

Select a phase: the GIS will show the corresponding countries and this panel will explain the related historical passage.
